ABOUT DEPARTMENT


The Electronics and Communication Engineering department established in the year 2003, offers Post Graduate program in Master of Technology in VLSI Design and Embedded Systems and Digital Electronics & Communication Systems at Post Graduate centers, Belagavi, Kalaburgi, Muddenahalli and Mysuru.

The department is equipped with well qualified and experienced faculty members and state-of-the-art laboratory Infrastructure facilities.

The department is committed to provide thorough understanding and application of core knowledge, Interpersonal and Communication skills, Analytical and Problem Solving skills,Creative thinking, Adaptability to rapid change in the Industry and Professional Ethics.
